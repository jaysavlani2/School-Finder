About The School

  • Education is an energetic force for the enlightenment of mankind. With the conception of this motto, Dholakiya Group of Schools has been concentrating on quality education for the last 44 years to fulfil the demand of knowledge society.

  • The visionary expertise of management differentiates the consistent performance of our students at National and International Level including Board Results, Science Fairs, IIT, IIM, CA CS, and many renowned institutes for higher studies. The throughout support of our parents and children have always made us No.1 in every field

    Our vision prioritizes collaborative and project based learning with in-depth conceptual clarity of global economy society to make a child an important member of vibrant  community of excellence enhances. The vision of our students to be life - long learners with knowledge, compassion, cheer and moral values. Our School Motto 'WE CARE' constantly inspires the learners teachers to turn small successes into bigger achievements.

    The School prepares students to understand, contribute to, and succeed in a rapidly changing society, thus making the world a better and more just place. We will ensure that our students develop both the skills that a sound education provides and the competencies essential for success and leadership in the emerging creative economy. We will also lead in generating practical and theoretical knowledge that enables people to better understand our world and improve conditions for local and global communities.
