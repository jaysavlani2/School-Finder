About The School

  • Innovative School is an institution holding secular and democratic values. We affirm our faith in the universal brotherhood of mankind and fatherhood of one God. The aim of the school is to develop the best that is latent in children & prepare them for the challenges of the changing world through encouragement of self discipline, high intellectual achievements and physical fitness.

  • We believe in inculcating the values of ‘Truth, Trust & Triumph’. The students are educated in a way that permits them to grow up without inhibition and prejudice in any form. Each & every student receives individual and personal attention and maximum possible comfort zone. It is our endeavor to make education purposeful, meaningful and enjoyable experience ensuring excellence in curricular & co-curricular activities.
