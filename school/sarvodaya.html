About The School

  • SARVODAYA EDUCATION NETWORK, launched in June 1995, is run by Shree Sarvodaya Education and Charitable Trust. This trust has truly earned the faith and confidence of the people of Rajkot because of their valuable contribution to the profession of teaching over the years.

Our Vision

  • When a child gets only a formal education, he becomes simply a machine but when he is exposed to almost all aspects of learning including 'training'. He becomes to be a real human and with such human, the Nation building takes place.

BURDENLESS EDUCATION

  • In contemporary education system students are not only force to lift heavy bags but also they are burdened with lot of mental stress about studies. To remove these hindrances sarvodaya has come up with worksheet system which caters to the needs of: Class work, Practices book, Science Journals, Map Work, Self Study Book and Note Books. The weight of all these books has been reduced and it is supplemented by subject wise and chapter wise worksheets, which are very easy to carry.

Our Mission

  • To offer integrated and holistic education to equip our students with the relevant skills, knowledge and self assurance, this will provide them with an effective platform for; the enhancement of their employability and for the future success in their career and personal life to become pride of the society and nation.
